Job Title: Junior Data Scientist
Location: Bangalore / Remote
Experience: 0–2 years
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role

We are looking for a Junior Data Scientist to join our analytics team.
The ideal candidate has strong foundations in Python, SQL, and data analysis,
and is eager to work on real-world machine learning problems.

Key Responsibilities

Clean, preprocess, and analyze structured and unstructured datasets

Develop basic machine learning models (classification, regression, clustering)

Build dashboards and reports to communicate insights

Work closely with senior data scientists and data engineers

Assist in developing, testing, and deploying predictive models

Document workflows, experiments, and results

Participate in code reviews and follow best practices

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends and patterns

Required Skills

Strong proficiency in Python

Experience with Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib/Seaborn

Solid understanding of SQL (queries, joins, aggregations)

Familiarity with scikit-learn for basic ML

Knowledge of statistics, probability, and hypothesis testing

Experience working with Git

Good communication and problem-solving skills

Preferred Skills (Good to Have)

Experience with Tableau or Power BI

Exposure to machine learning pipelines

Basic understanding of cloud platforms (AWS / GCP / Azure)

Knowledge of Streamlit for simple data apps

Familiarity with NLP basics

Educational Qualification

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, Engineering,
Mathematics, or a related field.

Benefits

Competitive salary

Mentorship from senior data scientists

Opportunity to work on real production ML systems

Health insurance, flexible working hours

Learning budget for courses and certifications
